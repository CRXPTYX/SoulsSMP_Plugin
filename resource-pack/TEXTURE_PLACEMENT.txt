TEXTURE PLACEMENT GUIDE
======================

Place your custom soul texture files (.png format, 16x16 pixels) in this directory:
resource-pack/assets/minecraft/textures/item/souls/

Required files:
- strength_soul.png
- mace_soul.png
- sea_soul.png
- resistance_soul.png
- regeneration_soul.png
- dash_soul.png
- absorption_soul.png
- haste_soul.png
- frost_soul.png
- vampire_soul.png
- phantom_soul.png
- saturation_soul.png
- ender_dragon_soul.png
- wither_soul.png
- warden_soul.png

After placing your textures:
1. Zip the entire resource-pack folder
2. Upload to a file hosting service
3. Configure the URL in config.yml
4. Reload the plugin with /souls reload
